About BIM Pars

BIM Pars is a Revit-based automation tool created to streamline and enhance workflows across all aspects of Building Information Modeling (BIM). It bridges the gap between design, model checking, and project optimization, making automation more accessible and efficient for BIM professionals. Whether you’re managing data, performing model checks, or improving project coordination, BIM Pars empowers you to handle complex tasks with ease, directly within the Revit environment.


Key Features

BIM Pars is built to provide you with the tools necessary to work smarter, not harder. It covers every aspect of your BIM workflow, from investigation to reporting, with a focus on efficiency, flexibility, and user-friendly automation. Here's what you can expect:

  1. Advanced Model Investigation
    Dive into your models with precision. BIM Pars allows you to apply advanced filters based on parameters, families, and project data, providing detailed insights that help you understand and navigate complex models with ease.
  2. Seamless Model Checking
    Perform accurate checks and comparisons across elements, parameters, and views within the model. BIM Pars ensures consistency and accuracy, helping you spot discrepancies early on and avoid costly errors down the line.
  3. Quick Parameter Editing
    Edit parameters directly within the app. BIM Pars gives you the flexibility to make changes quickly and efficiently, whether it’s fine-tuning data or adjusting values, all within a seamless workflow.
  4. Efficient Reporting
    Generate detailed reports effortlessly, and keep your documentation organized. BIM Pars automatically compiles the results, allowing you to edit, export, and share reports in multiple formats without leaving the app.
  5. Streamlined Collaboration
    Simplify communication and teamwork with in-app data storage and export options. Share saved reports and project data effortlessly without the risk of altering your model, ensuring smooth collaboration within the team.
  6. Custom Script Support
    BIM Pars allows you to store, execute, and manage your own custom scripts, with the added benefit of open-source flexibility. You can develop tailored solutions and even contribute to the tool’s evolution as part of a vibrant user community.
