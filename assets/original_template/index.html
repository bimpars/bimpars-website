What is BIM Pars?

A powerful Revit extension built within the PyRevit environment,
designed to streamline and optimize BIM workflows.

bim industry

BIM is transforming the construction landscape by enabling precise digital representations and efficient project management.

colaboration

Seamless collaboration ensures all stakeholders work from a unified model, reducing errors and enhancing teamwork.

model checking

Thorough model checking is crucial for identifying clashes and ensuring design accuracy before construction begins.

coordination

Effective coordination aligns data and resolves conflicts, streamlining workflows and minimizing on-site issues.

What comes next?

The evolution of BIM tools focuses on smarter automation and adaptability to meet the demands of complex projects.

What BIM PARS covers?

Efficient model analysis, automation, and collaboration with powerful
customization and open-source flexibility.

  • investigation

    Investigate complex models with advanced filters based on parameters, families, and project information.

  • check

    Perform checks and compare different elements, parameters, and views to ensure consistency and accuracy across the model.

  • edit

    Edit parameter values and customize reports directly within the app for greater control and flexibility.

  • report

    Automatically generate detailed reports for each task, edit them within the app, and export in various formats for professional documentation.

  • colaborate

    Facilitate teamwork with versatile export options, in-app data storage, and saved reports that can be easily shared without altering the model.

  • develop

    Store, execute, and manage your own custom scripts, and explore BIM Pars as an open-source tool for community development.
